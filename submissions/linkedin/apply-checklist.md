## Keyword coverage reminder

LinkedIn search and recruiter filters match on keywords across Headline, About, Experience, and Skills. Before finishing, confirm these terms each appear in **at least two** of those sections:

- **AI Engineering Leader / AI engineering leadership**
- **Agentic AI / Agentic development**
- **VP / Director** (seniority filter)
- **Developer productivity / developer velocity**
- **Claude Code, Codex, MCP, RAG** (tooling — recruiters search these by name)
- **Fractional / Advisory** (engagement shape)
- **gemba-core** (your unique, ownable term)
- **Atlanta + Remote** (location filters)

> Rule of thumb: if a recruiter would type it into a search box, it should live in your Headline or About *and* be reinforced in Experience or Skills. Front-load the top 3 so they survive truncation in search previews.
