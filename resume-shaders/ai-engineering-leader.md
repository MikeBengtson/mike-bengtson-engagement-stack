# AI Engineering Leader Resume Shader

## Target

VP / Director-level AI engineering leadership roles where the mandate is to operationalize agentic / AI-assisted development across an organization while staying hands-on in the codebase.

## Strategy

Lead with agentic-AI and developer-productivity impact, platform scale, and multi-squad leadership. Pair org-building (hiring, scaling squads, SDLC and release governance) with hands-on engineering. Surface gemba-core as proof of open-source, hands-on agentic tooling.

## Emphasize

- agentic AI and developer-productivity transformation (Claude Code, Codex, MCP, RAG, SpecKit, code-graph intelligence)
- gemba-core (open-source framework for parallel agentic development)
- platform scale (0 to 7M+ users; $175M AIDE AHT-savings target)
- multi-squad and chapter leadership; doubling engineering capacity under deadline
- measurable outcomes (~6x cycle-time reduction; nine-figure KPIs)
- AWS / Kubernetes / event-driven platform modernization

## De-Emphasize

- generic people-manager language with no hands-on signal
- early-career sales/quota detail except where it shows revenue ownership
- platform-specific keyword stuffing
