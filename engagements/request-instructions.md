# How To Send Mike An Engagement Request

Email is the primary intake surface: **mike.bengtson@gmail.com**.

Mike is an AI engineering leader (VP / Director level) and is open to four kinds of engagement — full-time (FTE), fractional / part-time leadership, contract / consulting, and advisory / board. The more clearly your request names the model, scope, and AI/agentic mandate, the faster it can be evaluated. See `current-preferences.md` and `engagement-models.md` for what he is looking for, and `evaluation-rubric.md` for how opportunities are weighed.

## Subject Line

Use this format:

```text
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] <Engagement Type> - <Organization/Person> - <Remote|Hybrid Atlanta|Onsite> - <Target Start>
```

Examples:

```text
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Full-Time VP Engineering - Acme AI - Remote - Q3 2026
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Fractional Head of Engineering - Northstar Labs - Remote - June 2026
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Consulting / Agentic SDLC Build - FinCo - Hybrid Atlanta - July 2026
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Advisory Retainer - FounderName - Remote - ongoing
```

## What To Include

- **Who you are** — your name, role, and how to reach you.
- **Organization or client** — and a one-line description.
- **Engagement type** — full-time, fractional / part-time leadership, contract / consulting, or advisory / board.
- **Role or scope** — the mandate, expected outcomes, and decision rights. Be explicit about how hands-on vs. purely managerial the role is.
- **The AI / agentic angle** — what role agentic / AI-assisted development plays, and how serious the organization is about adopting it. This is a primary fit signal.
- **Location model** — remote, hybrid (note that hybrid works best when local to Atlanta), or onsite; plus expected days in office if hybrid/onsite.
- **Time-zone expectations** — Mike is on US Eastern (ET); note any required overlap.
- **Start date and duration** — target start, and whether it is ongoing, fixed-term, or milestone-based.
- **Compensation or budget range** — please include your range or budget so the opportunity can be evaluated. Mike's own floors and targets are held privately; you do not need to know them, but a stated range or budget on your side speeds things up.
- **Decision process** — who decides, the steps, and the timeline.
- **Relevant links** — company, product, job post, or data room.
- **Why this may be a fit** — a sentence or two on why Mike specifically.

## Notes

- If required fields are missing, an agent may draft a clarification response before the opportunity is evaluated.
- Specific availability, start date, and any dealbreakers are not published here and may need to be confirmed in conversation.
- For a copy-paste request template (including an agent-readable YAML packet), see `request-email-format.md`.
