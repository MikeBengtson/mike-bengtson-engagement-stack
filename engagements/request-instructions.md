# How To Submit An Engagement Request

Email is the primary intake surface.

## Subject Line

Use this format:

```text
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] <Engagement Type> - <Organization/Person> - <Remote|Hybrid|Onsite> - <Target Start>
```

Examples:

```text
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Full-Time Staff Engineer - Acme AI - Remote - July 2026
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Fixed-Bid Build - Northstar Labs - Remote - June 2026
[ENGAGEMENT REQUEST] Advisory Retainer - FounderName - Hybrid NYC - Q3 2026
```

## Required Information

- Who you are
- Organization or client
- Engagement type
- Role or scope
- Location model
- Time-zone expectations
- Start date
- Duration
- Compensation or budget range
- Decision process
- Relevant links
- Why this may be a fit

If required fields are missing, an agent may draft a clarification response before evaluation.
