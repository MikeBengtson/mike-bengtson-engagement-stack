# Case Study

> Internal / NDA. Described at the level of publicly stated program scope only; no confidential architecture, data, or figures beyond the resume of record.

## Context

Fidelity's Consumer Portfolio Capability is an enterprise data-aggregation platform powering Planning & Guidance, real-time financial eventing, and bulk insights. When Mike joined (2022), the launch had stalled on performance and stability problems.

## Problem

Get a stalled, pre-production enterprise platform stable and into production, then scale it to serve millions of consumers reliably — while expanding what it could do.

## Constraints

- Highly regulated financial-services environment.
- A launch already stalled, with performance and stability issues blocking production use.
- Expectation to scale to millions of users on a live system with no room for regression.

## Approach

- **Diagnosed and stabilized** the performance and stability issues blocking production use — within two months of onboarding.
- **Reviewed the AWS architecture** (Micronaut Java, Aurora Postgres, SQS event bus) and drove the scale work: autoscaling, multi-region balancing, DB resizing, and rapid DR-failover tuning.
- **Took ownership as Chapter Leader**, owning roadmap and long-term strategy across business stakeholders, Product, and engineering.
- **Expanded scope** from consumer-spending aggregation to student-loan data, real-time credit-card spend eventing, and external investment-portfolio tracking with live pricing.

## Outcome

Scaled the platform **from 0 to 7M+ users in two years** as Engineering Chapter Leader, on a system live in production — turning a stalled launch into a multi-million-user capability.
