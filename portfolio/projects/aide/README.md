# AIDE (Fidelity Brokerage)

## Summary

**AIDE** is Fidelity Brokerage's AI "single pane of glass" servicing platform — consolidating 14+ servicing tools and dozens of back-end systems into one application for ~8,900 call-center associates, targeting **$175M in average-handle-time (AHT) savings over five years**. Mike led engineering for the AIDE program and the parallel Brokerage AI Innovation workstream that built the agentic-development practice powering it.

## Role

**Led engineering** — VP, Engineering Leader / Multi-Squad Chapter Leader for the AIDE program and Brokerage AI Innovation lead (2024–2026). Scaled the AIDE delivery org from 14 to 31 engineers in six months and shipped its highest-impact servicing capabilities. Evidence level: **led**.

## What This Demonstrates

- Leading a multi-squad engineering organization against a nine-figure business KPI ($175M AHT-savings target).
- Consolidating a sprawling servicing surface (14+ tools, dozens of back-end systems) into one associate-facing platform.
- Scaling a delivery org rapidly under deadline (14 → 31 engineers in six months) via internal, external, associate, and contingent hiring.
- Operationalizing agentic development to compress delivery cycles — a skill-based code-generation pipeline cutting some feature classes from ~3 months to a single two-week sprint (up to ~6×).
- Standing up an enterprise RAG + MCP knowledge layer and the cross-squad SDLC governance (integration testing, release management, AI-assisted review).
