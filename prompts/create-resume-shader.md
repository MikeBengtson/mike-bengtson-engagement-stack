# Create A Resume Shader

I want to create a resume shader for this Engagement Stack.

A resume shader is role/industry/platform-specific guidance for shaping the canonical CV into a scoped resume without editing canonical facts.

Read:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `cv/canonical-cv.md`
- `cv/work-history.md`
- `cv/skills.md`
- `portfolio/README.md`
- relevant `portfolio/projects/*`
- `evidence/README.md`
- `resume-shaders/README.md`
- `templates/resume-shaders/shader.template.md`
- `templates/resume-shaders/shader.template.yaml`

Then interview me about:

1. target role or industry;
2. target seniority;
3. target platform or submission channel;
4. projects and skills to emphasize;
5. material to de-emphasize;
6. language to prefer or avoid;
7. desired output formats.

Create:

- `resume-shaders/<shader-slug>.md`
- `resume-shaders/<shader-slug>.yaml`

Do not generate the final resume until I approve the shader.
