# Quickstart Interview

Use this when the user wants a minimum viable Engagement Stack in one session.

Ask:

1. What name, contact method, and public links should appear?
2. What one sentence should describe your professional identity?
3. What roles or engagements are you seeking now?
4. What are your three strongest work examples?
5. What proof links can we cite for those examples?
6. What location model do you prefer: remote, hybrid, or in-office?
7. What resume outputs do you need first?

Then populate the required files only.
