# Originals (preserved ground-truth)

Drop supplied, polished resumes here **verbatim**. Nothing in Engagement Stack
modifies these files — they are your canonical originals.

- Keep whatever format you received: `*.md`, `*.pdf`, `*.docx`.
- The active rendered variants live one level up as [`../short.md`](../short.md) and [`../long.md`](../long.md).
- Shaders and the renderer read the active variants and emit tailored copies under
  `generated/` or `submissions/`; they never touch this folder.

This folder is intentionally version-controlled (unlike `generated/`) so your
source-of-truth resumes are preserved alongside the rest of your stack.
